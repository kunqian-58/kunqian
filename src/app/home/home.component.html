Welcome to Kun's Personal Website

My name is Kun Qian ("钱坤" in Chinese). I am currently an Applied Scientist at Amazon Search Science and AI. Prior to joining Amazon, I was a Research Staff Member at IBM Research. My research is at the intersection of data integration, artificial intelligence, and NLP. Prior to joining IBM Research, I obtained my PhD in Computer Science under the supervision of Balder ten Cate, Phokion Kolaitis, and Wang-Chiew Tan at University of California Santa Cruz (UCSC). Before coming to the US, I also worked with James Cheng at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore.

Research & Publications

DBLP Page GoogleScholar Page

Research Interests: human-in-the-loop machine learning, active learning, semi-supervised learning, deep learning, explainable AI, data integration and data exchange, multilingual search.

My work at Amazon focuses on multilingual search. My research at IBM focused on data exchange and integration to, more recently, machine learning with small data. I have designed and developed several intelligent human-in-the-loop machine learning systems for entity-centric AI tasks. Representative systems include LUSTRE (ICDE 2018 demo, COLING 2018), SystemER (VLDB 2019 demo, CIKM 2017), and PARTNER (AAAI 2020 demo, EMNLP 2020).
